Data Co-Location Service

Overview

Adopting a data collocation strategy can significantly benefit your business, but selecting the right partner is key.

Hura's data collocation services enable you to securely host your IT infrastructure off-site in our state-of-the-art data centre, engineered for maximum uptime. By partnering with us, you can lower the capital and operational costs associated with housing and safeguarding your critical applications and systems.

We provide industry-leading Service Level Agreements (SLAs) that ensure reliability for power and environmental systems. Our redundant subsystems are designed to eliminate single points of failure, offering peace of mind and uninterrupted service. Hura's scalable collocation services allow you to adjust space, power, connectivity, and other services to match your evolving business needs.

Empower your business to streamline operations with Hura’s secure, flexible, and scalable data collocation services. We offer a variety of full-cabinet and multi-cabinet options to suit your enterprise's needs. Instead of dedicating resources to build and maintain your own data centre, let Hura provide the capacity and scalability you need as your business grows.
