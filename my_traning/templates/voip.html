With Hura VoIP service, you can easily configure your system to create the perfect impression for your customers. Whether your employees are located in different cities or even overseas, you can provide them with extensions on the same number or assign individual numbers with the same area codes as your main office.

Hura also offers a fully-featured telephone system, complete with capabilities like voice menus and extensions for specific team members. This is especially beneficial for corporate organizations, as it eliminates the high toll charges associated with traditional phone services.

Hura’s VoIP uses real-time protocol (RTP) to ensure that your communication packets are delivered promptly, even over public networks.
